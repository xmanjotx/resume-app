## 1. Smart Job Analysis

### What It Does
Automatically analyzes the job description you paste and extracts key information.

### Features

**Experience Level Detection**
- Identifies seniority level: Entry, Mid, Senior, or Executive
- Looks for keywords like "junior", "senior", "lead", "director"
- Helps you understand the role's position level

**Years of Experience Extraction**
- Automatically detects required years of experience
- Parses patterns like "5+ years" or "3 years"
- Shows exact requirement clearly

**Work Type Classification**
- Identifies if role is Remote, Hybrid, or On-site
- Looks for keywords like "remote", "work from home", "hybrid", "office"
- Helps with work-life balance planning

**Skill Detection**
- Extracts top 8 key skills from job description
- Identifies technical and soft skills
- Shows what the employer is looking for
- Helps you emphasize relevant skills in your resume

**Job Complexity Scoring**
- Calculates complexity based on job description length and requirements
- Rates as: Simple, Moderate, Complex, or Very Complex
- Helps you gauge the role's difficulty level

### Smart Insights
- Provides contextual tips based on the analysis
- Suggests focus areas for your resume
- Gives personalized recommendations

### Example Analysis
```
Job Description: "Senior Platform Engineer with 5+ years Kubernetes experience..."

Analysis Shows:
- Seniority: Senior
- Experience: 5+ years
- Work Type: Remote
- Complexity: Complex
- Key Skills: Kubernetes, Docker, CI/CD, AWS, Terraform, etc.

Smart Tip: "This is a senior-level position requiring 5+ years of experience. 
Focus on relevant achievements and quantifiable results."
```
